Kyligence

Kyligence Inc. is a leading data intelligence company focusing on Big Data technologies and innovation, offering intelligent platform and product powered by Apache Kylin. The company was founded by the team who created Apache Kylin, a powerful open source OLAP engine built for interactive analytics with petabyte-scale data on Hadoop. Kyligence is offering next generation data warehouse and business intelligence solution on top of Hadoop from on-premises to on-cloud.
